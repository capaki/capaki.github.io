ABOUT ME

Hello hello! My name is Candan. I’m 19. I am originally from Istanbul, Turkey but throughout my life, I’ve had the chance to live in a few different places, all of which have a special place in my mind. I guess that’s one of the reasons I love travelling so much, once you get a taste of it, you can’t stop. Even though you always leave a piece of your heart behind.

This is a website-blog where I will be writing about the places I’ve traveled, places I will travel and the places I wish to travel. I will try to share my personal experiences and hopefully, during the way, help or inspire you to get out of your comfort zone and go into the world.

If you would like to follow me on other platforms, you can always do so by clicking on the icons you can find at the bottom of every page.

Nice to meet you and welcome to my life.
